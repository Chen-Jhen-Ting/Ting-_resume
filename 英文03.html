Simpub  -- Ebook Platform Online Edit and Selling

In Simpub, users can search ebooks, donate the authors. Simpub support online reading and download books as PDF format. As an Author in Simpub, you can create your book promptly, update book's cover, add or remove chapter or section easily. Simpub support Markdown. To avoiding any accident, we save your update automatically. After completing your book, you can release your book Immediately. 

Online edit ebook -- edit books with Markdown and parse as HTML for online reading 

Convert PDF format -- convert online edit book in to PDF and save in AWS S3 

AWS-S3 -- manipulate objects in S3 via AWS Ruby SDK, saving or updating books content
